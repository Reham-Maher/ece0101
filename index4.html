Applications of artifitial intelligence :-

field Application
Healthcare AI can help doctors with diagnoses and can inform when patients are worsening so that medical help can reach to the patient before hospitalization.
education AI can automate grading so that the tutor can have more time to teach. AI chatbot can communicate with students as a teaching assistant.
Data Security AI can be used to make your data more safe and secure. Some examples such as AEG bot, AI2 Platform,are used to determine software bug and cyber-attacks in a better way.
E-commerce AI is helping shoppers to discover associated products with recommended size, color, or even brand.
Social Media AI can organize and manage massive amounts of data. AI can analyze lots of data to identify the latest trends, hashtag, and requirement of different users.
Agriculture AI is emerging in this field. Agriculture is applying AI as agriculture robotics, solid and crop monitoring, predictive analysis. AI in agriculture can be very helpful for farmers.
Astronomy Artificial Intelligence can be very useful to solve complex universe problems. AI technology can be helpful for understanding the universe such as how it works, origin, etc.
